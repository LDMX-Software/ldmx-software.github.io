Construct the DIGIs from energy and time simulated data.

Right now, it adds up the energies and finds an energy-weighted average of the times. These digis are then inserted into digiToAdd.

energies and times must be the same size. NO CHECKING IS DONE FOR THIS.

The ID of the digi samples is not set in this function. It is only meant to make the energy+time --> digis translation consistent and isolated.

Parameters
energiessimulated energy depositions
timessimulated times of energy depositions
digiToAddvector of EcalDigiSamples that will be filled with constructed digis
Returns
true if digiToAdd was actually filled with something
