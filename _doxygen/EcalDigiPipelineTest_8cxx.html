Test for the Ecal Digi Pipeline

Does not check for realism. Simply makes sure sim energies end up being "close" to output rec energies. Close is defined right now to be a maximum of 5% relative difference.

Checks

  • Keep reconstructed energy depositied within 5% of simulated value
  • Mark any pure noise hits as noise
