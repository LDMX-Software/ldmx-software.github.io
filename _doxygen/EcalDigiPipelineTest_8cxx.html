Test for the Ecal Digi Pipeline

Does not check for realism. Simply makes sure sim energies end up being "close" to output rec energies. Close is defined right now to be a maximum of 2.5% relative difference.

TODO still need to expand to multiple contribs in a single sim hit TODO check ability to discard noise / low energy sim hits TODO check layer weights are being calculated correctly somehow
