SimHit E Dep vs Rec Hit Amplitude

A perfect reconstruction would see a one-to-one linear relationship between these two. Integrates to number of Rec Hits. Aggregates EDeps from any SimHits in the same cell. The maximum is 25MeV.

Total Rec Energy
