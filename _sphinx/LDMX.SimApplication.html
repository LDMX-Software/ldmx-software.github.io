LDMX.SimApplication.generators.single_1pt2gev_e_upstream_tagger()¶

Configure a particle gun to fire a 1.2 GeV electron upstream of the tagger tracker.

This is used to study the rejection of off energy electrons in the tagger tracker. The position and direction are set such that the electron will be bent by the field and arrive at the target at approximately [0, 0, 0] (assuming it’s not smeared).

The gun position below requires the particles to be fired at 11.011 degrees. The direction vector is calculated as follows:

dir_vector = [ sin(11.011) = .2292/1.2, 0, cos(11.011) = 1.1779/1.2 ]

Returns

  • Instance of a particle gun configured to fire a single 1.2 Gev electron

  • directly upstream of the tagger tracker.
